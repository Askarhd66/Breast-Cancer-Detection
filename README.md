<h1>Breast Cancer Detection – AdaBoost & XGBoost</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
A fictional medical research team wanted to improve early detection of breast cancer using 
machine learning. Early diagnosis significantly increases survival rates, and the team needed 
a reliable model to classify tumors as benign or malignant.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Business Problem</h2>
<p>
Traditional diagnostic methods were slow and sometimes inconsistent. The team needed a 
high‑accuracy model that could support radiologists and reduce diagnostic errors.
</p>

<h2>🧠 What This Project Delivers</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Developed AdaBoost and XGBoost models for tumor classification.</li>
  <li>Achieved strong predictive performance on the WBCD dataset.</li>
  <li>Identified key tumor characteristics influencing predictions.</li>
  <li>Provided a decision‑support tool for medical professionals.</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠️ Techniques Used</h2>
<ul>
  <li>AdaBoost & XGBoost</li>
  <li>Feature Scaling & Cleaning</li>
  <li>Model Evaluation (Precision, Recall, ROC‑AUC)</li>
</ul>
